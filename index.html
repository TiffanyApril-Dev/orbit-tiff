Rusted Luxe – WordPress + WooCommerce E‑Commerce Build

Project Overview

Rusted Luxe is a Texas‑based horology and accessories brand blending bohemian freedom with steampunk grit and western elegance. My role was to design and build a fully branded e‑commerce experience using WordPress and WooCommerce. This included developing the visual identity, customizing the theme, adding products, configuring payments and shipping, and creating a cohesive, immersive shopping experience aligned with the brand’s unique personality.

Branding & Visual Identity

I established a cohesive branding system inspired by Texas landscapes and the brand’s “boho‑meets‑steampunk” aesthetic. The color palette combines muted earth tones with metallic accents, creating a bold yet grounded visual identity. Typography, graphics, and photography were selected to reinforce the contrast between rustic textures and refined luxury.

I also created a custom logo featuring a chic cowgirl in branded colors, wearing a watch around her waist. The typography intentionally contrasts: “Rusted” uses a rugged, industrial western font, while “Luxe” uses a bold, flowing script to communicate the brand’s core theme of grit meets glamour.

Product Setup & Catalog Structure

I added 32 products organized into four custom categories. Each product received pricing, weight, SEO/meta descriptions, mock inventory, and custom titles. I customized the product grid, category pages, and content backgrounds using branded overlays and opacity adjustments to maintain visual consistency across the site.

Cart, Checkout & Payment Integration

I personalized the cart and checkout pages to match the brand’s aesthetic and tested the full purchase flow in sandbox mode. I integrated WooPay for seamless native checkout and Google Pay for fast, one‑tap mobile payments. Automated tax calculation was enabled to ensure accurate, location‑based rates.

Shipping Setup

I implemented WooCommerce Shipping and migrated the store to the updated WooCommerce Shipping experience. Shipping rules included free shipping within the continental U.S., with pricing strategy adjusted to absorb part of the shipping cost.

Outcome

The final Rusted Luxe site delivers a cohesive brand identity, a fully functional WooCommerce store, custom product catalog, streamlined checkout, and a polished, story‑driven shopping experience. This project strengthened my skills in WordPress customization, WooCommerce configuration, branding, UX design, and e‑commerce implementation.
